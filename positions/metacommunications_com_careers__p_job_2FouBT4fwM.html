Software UI / UX Designer

MetaCommunications (Iowa City, IA)

Full Time

MetaCommunications is looking for a talented UI/UX Designer to create amazing user experiences in our industry-leading software products. The ideal candidate should have an eye for clean and elegant design, possess superior UI skills, be able to translate high-level requirements into interaction flows and artifacts, and transform them into intuitive and functional user interfaces.

Responsibilities:

  • Execute all visual design stages from concept to final hand-off to development
  • Bring simplicity and user friendliness to complex systems
  • Create wireframes, storyboards, user flows, process flows and site maps to effectively communicate interaction and design ideas
  • Analyze and develop user profiles with emphasis on user experience, human error control, display issues and visual interaction
  • Create, evaluate and modify designs to support evolving web and software application development
  • Collaborate with product team and developers to shape the product direction, visuals and user experience
  • Present and defend designs to peers and leadership group
  • Conduct user research and evaluate user feedback
  • Establish and promote design guidelines, best practices and standards

Need to have:

  • BA/BS in Design or Human Factors Engineering (graphic, user interaction, visual communications) or comparable professional experience and portfolio
  • 3-5 years UI design experience creating wireframes, storyboards, user flows, process flows and site maps
  • Expert knowledge of Photoshop, Illustrator, Balsamiq and other visual design and wire framing tools
  • Experience designing for platform specific UX functionality (Mobile, Responsive, etc.)
  • Strong communication, presentation and interpersonal skills
  • Curiosity and drive to solve problems creatively and effectively
  • Highly motivated, self-directed, and results driven
  • Ability to analyze problems, explain solutions, and present technical solutions to end-users in a non-technical manner

Nice to have:

  • Experience in HTML, CSS and JavaScript for rapid prototyping
  • Experience working on a team developing enterprise software solutions
  • Experience working in a SaaS company
  • Experience collaborating with cross-functional teams
